Affan Al Nassar

★★★★★

"The Finest Burger in Dhaka The Gourmet Beef Burger at Comptoirs Richard. It's a french cafe with high ceilings, well spaced tables and a quiet atmosphere. It's the kind of spot where I half-expect someone to order a croissant and read a newspaper. I was there for their gourmet beef burger which has been getting some traction online as the best burger in Dhaka. The burger arrives with a side of fries and ketchup. The patty is so amazingly juicy, not the kind that seeps out when you press it, but the kind where it's pooling up on your plate just after separating the halves. The beef is so deeply flavourful, but somehow not gamey at all. Whoever chose the blend knew what they were doing. I don't write much, I don't have the skills necessary to explain the euphoria I was experiencing. The bun was fresh and airy with a nice light chew to it. I don't know much about baking. I absolutely love how this burger is assembled with care. This burger trusts its meat. No sauce overload, no towers of toppings. Most places in Dhaka dress their burgers like they're hiding something— their ugly grey flat discs. It's just so effortlessly confident. The fries aren't anything too special they tasted good and had a nice crisp to them. Good shape and size. The price is definitely quite high at 1175tk no doubt, but it's so worth it. I wish I made enough bank to eat this every other day. The amount of fries could be more. Get the burger, everything else is just context."

Dewan Asif

★★★★★

"This French restaurant in Dhaka, arguably the best in Bangladesh, serves some of the finest French cuisines available. Starting with appetizers, the Tenderloin Beef Cuts is a must-try—juicy, tender beef served with French bread cubes, blending perfectly with every bite bursting with flavors. The Smoked Chicken & Cheese Sandwich is another standout, with a rich, delicious cheese flavor. Don’t miss their Cream of Mushroom Soup—hands down the best mushroom soup, paired with perfectly toasted garlic bread. For mains, their Creamy Chicken Pasta and Spaghetti Bolognese are perfectly cooked and full of flavor. The Sirloin Steak is soft, juicy, and seasoned to perfection, while the Bone Marrow Burger offers an explosion of mouthwatering spices and rich bone marrow. The Harissa Chicken Meal is another great option for savory lovers. In desserts, the Caramelized Apple steals the show—caramelized apple slices atop buttered toasted bread with ice cream make for the perfect sweet treat. Crème Brûlée and Death by Chocolate are also highly recommended. The coffee here is aromatic, with expertly roasted beans and a variety of flavors. For drinks, the Mint Mojito is wonderfully refreshing. The ambience is cozy, and the staff provides excellent service. Special mention to Mr. Sani for his politeness and cheerful demeanor. Highly recommended as the best French dining experience in Dhaka!"
